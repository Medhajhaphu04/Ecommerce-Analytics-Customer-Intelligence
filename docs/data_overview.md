# Dataset Overview

## Dataset Name

Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist

---

## Objective

The dataset contains information about customers, orders, products, payments, reviews, and sellers from a Brazilian e-commerce platform.

The objective of this project is to build an **AI-Powered E-Commerce Analytics & Customer Intelligence Platform** capable of generating business insights, customer segmentation, sales forecasting, and executive recommendations.

## Key Business Entities

### Customers

Stores customer information and location details.

### Orders

Stores order lifecycle information and timestamps.

### Order Items

Stores products purchased within each order.

### Payments

Stores payment methods and payment values.

### Products

Stores product metadata and product characteristics.

### Reviews

Stores customer satisfaction scores and review comments.

### Sellers

Stores seller information and geographical details.
